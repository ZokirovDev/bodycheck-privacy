Privacy Policy

BodyCheck: BMI & Health Tracker

Last updated: June 17, 2026

BodyCheck (“the App,” “we,” “our”) is an independently developed application. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. By using BodyCheck, you agree to the terms described below.

BodyCheck is built as an offline-first health calculation tool. There is no account registration, and we do not operate any backend server that stores your personal health data.

Information we do not collect

BodyCheck does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following on remote servers:

  • Your name, email address, or account credentials
  • Your height, weight, age, gender, body measurements, or any health calculation inputs and results
  • Your location
  • Your device contacts, photos, or files outside the App’s own storage

All health data you enter (height, weight, BMI history, water intake logs, weight goals, and similar) is stored locally on your device only, using Android’s local database and preference storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share or export it yourself, using the App’s native share or CSV export feature, which is entirely under your control.

If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is permanently deleted from your device.

Information collected by third-party services

BodyCheck displays advertisements through Google AdMob, a service provided by Google LLC. AdMob may automatically collect certain technical and usage information in order to deliver and measure advertisements, which may include:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, operating system version, language settings)
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it
  • App usage and interaction data related to ad display

This information is collected and processed by Google according to Google’s own privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy. We do not control how Google processes this data.

Your advertising choices. You can opt out of personalized advertising through Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization on your Android device.

If you are in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, AdMob requests your consent for personalized advertising in compliance with the GDPR through a consent dialog on first launch. If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information; since BodyCheck itself does not sell or share personal data, this primarily concerns data processed by Google AdMob.

Notifications

BodyCheck may send local notifications, such as water intake reminders or weekly progress summaries, generated entirely on your device from data already stored locally. No notification content is created by or sent to any remote server. You can disable these at any time in the App’s Settings or your device’s system notification settings.

Permissions used by the App

PermissionPurpose
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Shows local reminders (water intake, weekly reports). Optional — denying it does not affect core app functionality.
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Restores scheduled local reminders after your device restarts.

BodyCheck does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or location, and only accesses storage as needed for its own local data and the optional image or CSV sharing features.

Data security

Since your health data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server we control, its security depends on your device’s own protections. We recommend keeping your operating system updated and using a secure lock method.

Children’s privacy

BodyCheck is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can address it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes in the App’s features or applicable law. The “Last updated” date above will be revised accordingly. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding personal data processed by third parties such as Google AdMob, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing, and to withdraw consent. Since BodyCheck does not store your personal data on any server, requests regarding third-party ad data should generally be directed to Google via the controls referenced above.
